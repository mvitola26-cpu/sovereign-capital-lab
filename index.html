What We Offer

Your Path to Consistent Profitability

Everything you need — from first trade to funded account — structured into a clear, repeatable process.

01

Structured Curriculum

A sequenced trading education covering technical analysis, market structure, chart reading, and trading psychology. Each module builds on the last — no random YouTube rabbit holes.

02

1-on-1 Mentorship

Weekly sessions with an active mentor who reviews your trade journal, delivers personalized feedback, and holds you to a consistent process. This eliminates the emotional decisions that wipe most traders out.

03

Prop Firm Challenge Prep

We prepare you specifically for funded account challenges (FTMO, MyFundedFX, etc.) — simulating challenge rules during training so you're ready to pass and protect real capital from day one.

04

AI-Assisted Trade Journal

Daily trade reviews powered by AI supervision to identify behavioral patterns — overtrading after losses, entering too early, revenge trading — before they cost real money.

05

Risk Management Framework

Never risk more than 1–2% per trade. Hard stop-losses on every position. Size trades based on account balance, not emotion. This framework prevents the account-destroying mistakes most beginners make.

06

Long-Term Wealth Building

Learn to allocate trading profits into index funds, ETFs, and dividend stocks — building compound wealth across the full financial lifecycle, not just during active trading sessions.
